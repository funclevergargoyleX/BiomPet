About the Team

We’re a locally based business in the city of Palo Alto. Over the years, we first started out rough, having no ideas of products we were going to make. Recently, we had a breakthrough. We have constructed a product which is almost perfect in every way for pets. This product is the BiomPet Collar. A collar which can track your pet’s health, heart rate, what it needs to eat, what it’s feeling, everything you could think of, even a camera. We have grown extremely from when we first started out, from having no ideas and not even knowing if we should continue, to having a strong foundation and allies to support. Before this all happened, we hadn’t known each other yet, though one day, at college, we met somehow.
